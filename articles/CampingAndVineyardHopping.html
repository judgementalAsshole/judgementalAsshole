last weekend, 2022

Palisade, CO
Camping and Vineyard Hopping

As much as I feel like a sucker for paying to sleep outside,
this campsite was worth it. Fortunately, I was able to drown my inner
cheapskate with lots of wine, lots and lots of wine!

Let me preface this article by saying that I'm more of a 'beer drinking, dive bar' kind of guy, but what the hell?! I had a crazy hair growing out of my ass and decided to go to the Colorado wine country over on the western slope of the state. What's even weirder is that I went by myself. A straight, white male who's single. Forgive me for stereotyping, but I'm not exactly the target demographic for that locale.

So here's a table of the booze makers I visited:
