About

Indulge me...
And allow me to present my qualifications, or a vague semblance of them.

Just kidding, I don't have any qualifications to present. I'm just some random, judgemental asshole on the internet who likes to speak their mind... and I've noticed anyone in this world who speaks their mind is considered an asshole. No matter who they are, whether they are male or female, gay or straight, religious or atheist, left-handed or right-handed, black or white or brown or any shade in between. Anyone brave enough to exercise their inalienable right to freedom of speech is just considered an asshole. So fuck it! I'm gonna own that fact and speak my mind in the form reviews and travel blogging.

Now, don't get me wrong, I don't embrace assholery for sake of it. I'm quite frankly a relatively positive person and for the most part I will give positive reviews. Sometimes I might put a weird spin on things, but that's my perverse sense of humor. Sometimes I'll have a bad experience and I'll passionately express my discontent. So read it or ignore it. The internet is a big space, and at the end of the day I'm doing this as more of a Jungian exercise for myself.

Anyway, much love and respect to you. I hope you find some truth and value in what I have to write.

All the best,
Some judgemental asshole on the internet
