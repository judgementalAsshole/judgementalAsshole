About

Indulge me...
And allow me to present my qualifications, or vague semblance of them.

Just kidding, just like any other critic in the world, my qualifications don't give me any license to be an asshole. No one, in my humble opion, should feel licensed to be an asshole. Anyway, thanks for letting me get that off my chest. I don't actually plan to be an asshole. I just chose the name because it has that attention grabbing pizzazz... low-brow? perhaps, but get over it.

So, what qualifies me? ummmmm, good question. I'm just an asshole on the internet who knows a little bit about a lot of different things. A jack-of-all-trades and master at none, so the saying goes. I'm mildly interesting at parties. I can speak a few languages. I've seen how both sides live. I've slept in city parks (willingly and unwillingly), but I've also been pampered in the finest of Tahitian beaches and Ritz Carltons. I've been to 6 out of 7 continents (one day I'll step foot on Antartica, but not high on my bucket list).

What else can I say? Not sure really. I guess the point I'm trying to make is that I have some decent perspective, or at least as much as a dipshit like me can have. Anyway, as much as I may sound like an asshole, it's not without some self-deprecation and personal reflection... So lighten up and enjoy some candid travel tales and whatnot.

Oh yeah... I like writing with words, so if you hate that then I don't recommend this site for you.
